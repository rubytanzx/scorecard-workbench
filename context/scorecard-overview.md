# IDA Scorecard — Overview

The WBG Corporate Scorecard FY24-FY30 tracks World Bank Group results across three pillars:

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Vision | Global development trends — not attributable to WBG |
| Client Context | Country-level conditions in WBG borrower countries |
| Results | Direct beneficiaries of IBRD, IDA, IFC, MIGA operations |

This codebase builds the IDA Scorecard surfacing results for IDA-eligible countries, with lenses for FCS, LDCs, and Small States/SIDS.

- Scorecard period: FY24-FY30
- Board-endorsed: Dec 19, 2023
- Current reporting year: FY25, ending June 30, 2025
